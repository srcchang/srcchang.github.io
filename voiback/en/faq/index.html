Frequently Asked Questions

What is VOIBACK?

VOIBACK is a party voice challenge game. You record a phrase, the app reverses it, and you have to mimic that reversed sound — hilarious whether you succeed or fail!

Do I need an internet connection?

Nope! VOIBACK works completely offline. All audio processing happens locally on your device. No internet required.

What languages are supported?

VOIBACK supports 5 languages from day one: 繁體中文, 简体中文, English, 日本語, 한국어. The interface and phrase library switch automatically.

Does it have ads?

VOIBACK is free with banner ads and occasional interstitials. You can purchase a one-time IAP to remove ads permanently.

Is the scoring accurate?

The score is designed for fun, not precision. We use envelope similarity + pitch contour analysis. A bit of randomness adds to the party vibe!
