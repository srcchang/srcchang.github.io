Why Choose Drive Pass?

A scientific approach to help you study as efficiently as possible

SM-2 Smart Review

Based on the SuperMemo spaced repetition algorithm, the app automatically schedules the optimal time to review each question. Questions you get wrong appear more frequently, while questions you get right are spaced further apart — so you spend your time where it matters most.

Pass Rate Prediction

Your real-time pass rate is calculated by combining your mock exam scores, practice accuracy, and question bank coverage. No more guessing whether you're ready — the numbers tell you.

Daily Challenge & Pre-Exam Sprint

10 questions every day to keep you sharp. Activate Sprint Mode before your exam, and a three-tier recommendation engine automatically selects the questions you need most.

Mock Exam

100% faithful to the real exam format — timed, auto-submitted, with per-question review afterward. A full simulation so you walk in confident on exam day.

Wrong Answer Notebook

Automatically saves every question you answer incorrectly. Filter, sort, and redo them in bulk. Paired with the SM-2 algorithm to precisely target your weak spots.

Achievement System

14 achievement badges to keep you motivated. Track your growth across questions practiced, consecutive correct answers, high mock exam scores, and daily streaks.
